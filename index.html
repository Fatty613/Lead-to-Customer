Zoho CRM: Lead to Customer Management

This process flow illustrates the journey of a lead within Zoho CRM, from initial capture through qualification and conversion, culminating in comprehensive customer record management. This is designed using out-of-the-box functionalities to ensure efficient sales operations and a unified customer view.
