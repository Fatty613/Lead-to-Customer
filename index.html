Key Improvements & Automation

Automated Pricing & Discounts

The system automatically applies the correct Sterling (UK) or Euro (EU) price list and customer-specific discounts, eliminating manual errors and accelerating the quote process.

Real-Time Inventory Visibility

Gain a live, accurate view of stock levels across all warehouses. Stock is adjusted automatically as components are bundled or goods are dispatched, preventing overselling.

Streamlined Customs Invoicing

A fully automated process generates customs-compliant commercial invoices, consolidating multiple shipments and including all necessary data like commodity codes and weights for rapid clearance.
